About Lead Sources

Lead sources help you track where your leads are coming from and measure the effectiveness of your marketing efforts.

Source Types:
  • Website: Direct website inquiries
  • Referral: Customer or partner referrals
  • Social Media: Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.
  • Email: Email marketing campaigns
  • Advertisement: Paid advertising (Google, Facebook, etc.)
  • Event: Trade shows, webinars, conferences
  • Cold Outreach: Cold calls, cold emails
  • Other: Any other source type
Tip: Set a cost per lead to track your marketing ROI and compare source effectiveness.
